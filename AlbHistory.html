A Brief History
Year Date Event
1190 -- The Principality of Arbanon was created.
1200 -- Local Albanian noble families begin to emerge
1258 May The Angevin Kingdom of Sicily seized the Albanian coast and much of the Epirus hinterland.
1272 June Forces allied to Angevin Charles I of Naples occupied Durrës and established the Kingdom of Albania.
1280 July Siege of Berat (1280–1281): An Angevin offensive against Constantinople was repulsed by the Byzantines.
1290 December Albania began to come under Serbian attack
