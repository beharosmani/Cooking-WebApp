Pite Me Mish

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g minced meat
  • 1 large onion
  • small portion of salt
  • small portion of pepper
  • small portion of oil

INTRUCTIONS

  1. In a large dish, we put the flour first..
  2. We make a hole with our hands in the middle of flour and we put the oil and salt there
  3. Then add the water slowly while mixing until the entire meal is included in the mixture and become a dough which is divided during mixing by hand.
  4. This dough we divide into two parts and let stand 20 minutes to join the best ingredient and becomes elastic dough
  5. We put the meat in a saucepan and we put a bit of a oil there
