Qebapa

INGREDIENTS

  • 1kg flour
  • 500ml water
  • 200gr oil
  • 1 spoon salt
  • 1kg spinach
  • 100gr cheese
  • 5 spoons yogurt
  • 1 egg
  • 2-4 new onions
  • 1 spoon salt

INTRUCTIONS

  1. We put the meat and some minced onion in a blender.
  2. Salt and black pepper, red pepper (ground pepper seeds), and sparkling water.
  3. We blend it all together, and we leave it in the refrigerator until we prepare pitalka.
