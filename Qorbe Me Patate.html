Qorbe Me Patate

INGREDIENTS

  • boiled beef
  • 1kg potatoes
  • salt
  • dry menderz
  • 1 bay leaf

INTRUCTIONS

  1. We put the meat on a pan full of water.
  2. We prepare the potatoes by washing and peeling them . We also add some carrots
  3. Prepare some vegetables in a non-stick pan with a little oil ,until the have taken a very light brown color.
  4. Add some salt,pepper.
  5. Once the meat has been boied, put the vegetables in the pot ,and also add some watter and some bay leaf.
  6. Continue simmering until the potatoes are softened
