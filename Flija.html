Flija

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 peppers
  • 300g frozen minced meat
  • 300g rice
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • fresh minced dill
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • black pepper

INTRUCTIONS

  • The dough should be baked individually heated in a coal saç.
  • Once a sequence stream of dough in pan, should be placed on baking-pan. Each sequence of it should be smeared again with melted cream
  • Fill peppers with this composition and the upper set as hats.
  • We put the pan by aligning and distribute the remaining part around peppers. We add 1-2 cups of water and bake for about 20-30 minutes, or until rice and peppers to be softened, adding water if necessary.
