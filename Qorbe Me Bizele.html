Pite Me Mish

INGREDIENTS

  • 500g peas
  • 200g beef
  • 1 onion
  • 300g tomato sauce
  • 1 carrot

INTRUCTIONS

  1. We peel the onion and put in a saucepan and add some olive oil.
  2. We also add some carrots and start preparing the peas.
  3. And finally throw the tomatoes or sauce,spices,oregano pepper.
  4. We put some water in the saucepan,and put it in the oven and for 30 min in enough heat it will be prepared.
