Lakra

INGREDIENTS

  • 7 pieces of meat
  • 1 cabbage
  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • tomato sauce
  • salt
  • red pepper
  • oil

INTRUCTIONS

  1. First we peel the onion,and cut the carrot
  2. In the pot where the cabbage will be boiled, we put oil and chopped vegetables.
  3. Then add the pieces of meat, cover the pot and into them prepare slowly.
  4. Then we take cabbage and clean it up and cut it in small pieces, and we add it to the pot with also vegetables and meat and add three cups of water.The pot should be covered and we let them be boiled until they turn red.
