Pite Me Spinaq

INGREDIENTS FOR PETE

  • 1kg flour
  • 500ml water
  • 200gr oil
  • 1 spoon salt

INGREDIENTS FOR THE FILLING

  • 1kg spinach
  • 100gr cheese
  • 5 spoons yogurt
  • 1 egg
  • 2-4 new onions
  • 1 spoon salt
